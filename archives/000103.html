Descendants of Frederick and Mary Brinker

If you have found this page, then you were looking for a common relative.
The listing below contains the descendants of Frederick and Mary Brinker.
Frederick Mary Brinker are not one of my sixteen sets of my great-great-great grandparents.
Their parents would be part of the sixteen sets. I know little about Frederick Mary.
Their child John Charles Brinker was extremely secretive about his past; and when he did speak of his birth and parents, he sometimes provided contradictory information. He found it difficult to keep his story straight. The name of his parents was provided on his first marriage license. The last name of his mother was found on his death certificate and was like provided by his son. I do believe that John was born in Pennsyvania and I have found a possible listing in the 1870 census for a boy that could be him. If it is him, he is not living with his parents or his parents are not Frederick and Mary.
You may wish to visit my family tree index for a complete listing of both ancestors and descendants.
Contact me, if you find an error that you want corrected or if you want to contribute new information.
