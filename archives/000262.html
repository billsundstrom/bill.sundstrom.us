Birthday Party

Celebrating 70 years of Life

To all my friends and family
Please, join us on the 8th of July

I will be 70 years-old in July. It has been 10 years since my last birthday party. The time has come for old friends to gather on a hot summer day. Bring a friend and your families. This grandfather loves children and they will be welcome. So join the celebration.

Details -

Where:
Our backyard at the Elliot Avenue house in Bloomington.

When:
Saturday, the 8th of July from 5 to 11 pm.

Refreshments:
We will have soft drinks, beer, wine and some stronger spirits.
Or bring your favorite beverage or something special to share.

Food:
We will have Mexican food for all.
You are welcome to bring a dish or dessert. If you do, please, contact Betty.

History:
From my 20th to my 60th annual birthday party, the infamous celebration led to much fun.
Do you remember that special birthday cake from my 30th, or the roasted pig from my 60th.
Bring a story from a bygone party, a memory to share. And let us create a new memory on the 8th of July.
